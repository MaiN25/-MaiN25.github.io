Runnin' In Space

About the game

I created a 3D Platformer project - Runnin' In Space while participating in the MSU Game Design and Development with Unity 2020 Specialization. I developed this game using the assets and guidance given during the lectures.

The modifications I added or chnage in the game are the following

  • Added level 3.
  • Created a new map.
  • Modified the levels made during the lectures.
  • Added new enemies (exploding bots and robots).
  • Added additional functionality to the new enemies, the robots deal double the damage done by the regular enemies, and the exploding bots deal triple the damage done by the regular enemies

Game Instructions

  • The player controls would be both the keyboard arrows and WSAD.
  • The player would jump once you press the ENTER key.
  • To kill the enemies, you should jump on their heads.
