About the game

I completed this game as part of AET 319 course at UT Austin. The game's code and mechanism were provided to us by the course instructors. Our task was to develop a game that conveyed a narrative solely through visual and subtle storytelling techniques without modifying the provided code nor mechanism nor adding UI elements. The game's storyline follows Angie, an optimistic angel who sets out on a mission to reach heaven. She navigates through a challenging obstacle course in hell to reunite with her loved ones in heaven. Join us in helping Angie on her journey!
