Space Blaster

About the game

I created a 2D Shooter project - Super Space-Stronaut while participating in the MSU Game Design and Development with Unity 2020 Specialization. I developed this game using the assets and guidance given during the lectures.

The modifications I added or chnage in the game are the following

  • Added three more levels.
  • Modified the level made during the lectures.
  • Added new enemies and environment challenges
  • modified the speed of the player.
  • Added a lives bar

Game Instructions

  • The player is controled by your mouse
  • The player will shoot once you press the right click on the mouse
