Forest Of Wonders

Embark on a magical quest and collect the 10 enchanted mushrooms scattered throughout the mystical world to complete the ultimate challenge! Can you find them all and unlock the secret of their powers?

About the game:

Forest of Wonder is a captivating game that invites players to embark on a magical quest and explore a mystical world filled with challenges and wonder. As the sole developer and designer of the game, I used my skills in Unity and expertise in design principles to create a unique and engaging experience for players. Players must collect ten enchanted mushrooms scattered throughout the game's various environments to complete the ultimate challenge and unlock the secrets of their powers. The game mechanics are designed to provide players with a sense of discovery and achievement as they navigate through fantastical landscapes and find the hidden mushrooms. One of the unique selling points of Forest of Wonder is its original and imaginative world design. The game features meticulously crafted landscapes and customized non-copyrighted 3rd party models that I modified so they would fit into the game's overall aesthetic. I also developed and designed the UI elements to ensure a consistent and immersive experience for players. With Forest of Wonder, I aim to appeal to gamers who love exploring magical worlds and engaging in unique gameplay experiences. The game's potential target audiences include fantasy fans, puzzle enthusiasts, and casual gamers looking for an enjoyable and immersive experience. I am proud of my work in Forest of Wonder, and I believe it has amazing potential due to its unique gameplay mechanics and captivating world design.
